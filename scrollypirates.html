Travels

We've got a load of worldworld ports for pirates to voyage to. We arrange everything, you sit on board, drink yer rum ration and when yer get there, yer do yer stuff.

Arizona Dramatic High Points

yer get yer bike and yerself on these roads; Mount Lemmen, Prescot Skull Valley, Sedona Heights, Route 66 and the road to and into Grand Canyon! Andalusian Spain - Based north of Granada, we get your bike and/run stronger in a week! You can get 10,000 meters (30,000 feet) of Vuelta routes in a few days! Guaranteeed sore legs if you wish OR if your pirate in spirit isn't so energised, there's yoga, massage, painting and just hanging out in the quiet, scenic countryside.

Pyrennes, France

based on the Tour de France famous Tourmalet, you'll get to ride many of the TdF's climbs and return fatigued, but happy. Everest Base Camp Trek. No, this is not the trashed south-side, this is the longer, more scenic and Tibet-based north-side of Everest! You get to trek in with Sean James (twice summiteer and mountain guide) and his expedition next April/May. It's a toughie, but it really is a once in a lifetime opportunity.
